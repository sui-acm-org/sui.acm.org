Call for Sponsors

ACM SUI 2018 is going be the 6th edition of the Symposium on Spatial User Interaction. This autumn, SUI will be held in Berlin, Germany.

SUI is the premiere venue to disseminate the latest results in Spatial User Interaction research. The themes championed by SUI are highly prominent in today’s world, as the means to support tasks occurring in the space we live in require cutting edge technologies. SUI attracts an active community of academics, students, and industry professionals.

As the Organizing Committee we are committed to ensure that you, our sponsor, receive exposure to our community with many different advertisement opportunities. We look forward to support you with the benefits listed below. We are also open to discussing specific needs and opportunities tailored to meet your specific needs.

Previous SUI sponsors include companies like Google or NS Solutions, foundations like the Interaction Design Foundation and Tateisi Science and Technology Foundation, as well as many dedicated societies, i.e., RSJ, JSAI, VRSJ, etc., or research institutions, i.e., EIC (Japan), ITE (Japan) or USC (USA).

We want to give start-ups, studios and small enterprises the opportunity to participate. Ask us for special conditions.

Sponsorship Opportunities

SUI provides three levels of sponsorship: Gold, Silver, and Bronze. Sponsors at the Gold and Silver will have space in the break area of the venue. For all inquiries, please contact the Industry Chair.

All sponsors will receive:

  • A listing of your company (with logo and URL) in all official announcements, on the advance program, in the proceedings, and on the symposium web pages.
  • The placement of a corporate logo, with acknowledgement of sponsorship level, on the signs at room entrances in the symposium venue.
  • An acknowledgement and recognition of thanks for your company’s contribution at the banquet dinner and the opening and closing sessions of the symposium (as part of the organisation slide presentation).
  • Sponsorship announcement on SUI’s social media accounts.

Gold - 5000€ or greater

  • 1 page advertisement in the program book.
  • Possibility of providing materials and give-away items for the attendees.
  • Exhibition space in the break area.
  • Opportunity to name an event after your company. There will be a banquet, a reception, and various coffee breaks.
  • Opportunity to grant your own award, e.g., “Best Demo Award”.
  • 2 free registrations for people from your company to attend the conference

Silver - 2500€ or greater

  • 1/2 page advertisement in the program book.
  • Possibility of providing materials and give-away items for the attendees.
  • Exhibition space in the break area.
  • After Gold sponsors, you will have the possibility of naming events after your company. There will be a banquet, a reception, and various coffee breaks.
  • After Gold sponsors, you will have the opportunity to grant your own award, e.g., “Best Demo Award”.
  • 1 free registration for people from your company to attend the conference.

Bronze - 900€ or greater

  • Logo printed in the program book.
  • Possibility of providing materials and give-away items for each attendee.
  • 1 free registration for people from your company to attend the conference

Tailor-made opportunities

Tailor-made sponsorship is intended to be a flexible alternative to the three sponsoring levels described above. Please contact Thomas Jung, our sponsoring chair, to find the best solution for your marketing purposes. Some examples of tailor-made options are:

  • Exhibition spaces
  • Recruitment desk
  • Sponsoring of specific events at SUI (banquet, student volunteer event, etc.).
  • Sponsoring of prizes for Best Paper, Best Poster, and Best Demo awards.
  • Symposium merchandise, i.e., bag, pen, t-shirts, etc., with your logo.
  • etc...
