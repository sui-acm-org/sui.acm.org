Accessibility

SUI values the diversity of our community and aims to be an accessible and inclusive conference. If you have any questions or concerns about accessibility, please contact the conference accessibility chairs at local@sui.acm.org, and they will work with you to address your concerns. Note that some requests may require advance notice, so please submit accessibility requests as far in advance of the conference date as possible in order to give us adequate time to prepare. The conference hotel offers the following:

  • Barrier-free access to all conference rooms
  • Two parking spots reserved for disabled parking
  • Two wheelchair-accessible rooms

Children and Care Givers

We have options for child care. As this is very specific to the age of the child, the languages spoken and the need, please drop us an email to local@sui.acm.org and we are happy to find a good solution. When you register yourself, you can also register children and/or a care giver (for yourself or for the child).

  • Children (10€) — registered children are welcome to attend any SUI event, including the Conference Banquet. No drink tickets will be included with child registration. You do not have to register your child, of course, but if you want to bring them to any of the events, they have to be formally registered so that they are covered by the ACM insurance.

  • Care Givers (40€) — this registration category is for people providing care to the conference attendees with impairments or to the children of Conference Banquet. It grants the person access to all SUI events except the Conference Banquet. No drink tickets will be included with the care giver registration. If you would like your care giver to attend the Banquet, you will be able to purchase an additional Banquet ticket when you register your care giver.

Student Funding

Gary Marsden Student Development Fund

In recognition of Gary Marsden’s contributions and inspiration in HCI4D and support of HCI in the developing world, ACM SIGCHI established the Gary Marsden Student Development Fund in 2015. This fund is especially intended for sponsoring postgraduate students (Master or PhD degree) from and currently based in developing countries to attend SIGCHI (co)sponsored conferences as well as other HCI relevant conferences. In the last one year, 21 students were awarded by the fund. (More details)

Deadlines: 15th of February, May, August and November.

SIGCHI Student Travel Grant

The SIGCHI Student Travel Grant (SSTG) program is intended to enable students who lack other funding opportunities to attend SIGCHI-sponsored or co-sponsored conferences. This travel grant is intended to support students whose intention is to present at a SIGCHI-sponsored conference, not just attend. (More details)

Venue

This year SUI will take place at the Park Inn By Radisson Alexanderplatz Berlin, Germany. Found in the heart of Berlin and just around 250m from this year's UIST conference located at the nearby Berlin Congress Center, the hotel offers picturesque views over the capital's skyline. For our conference participants, 50 reservations have been made including a special offer. Please ask for ACM SUI rates during the reservation process.


How to Arrive

Air

From Berlin-Tegel Airport TXL

Change to stop of bus line “TXL”. Leave the bus at the station Hauptbahnhof. Take S-Bahn line S5 heading towards Strausberg Nord, S7 heading towards Ahrensfelde,Wartenberg or S9 heading towards Berlin-Schonefeld to Alexanderplatz station. Journey time approx. 30 min.

From Berlin-Schonefeld Airport SXF

Change to Berlin-Schonefeld railway station. Take railway line RE7 heading towards Dessau and leave at Alexanderplatz station. Alternatively take S-Bahn line S9 heading towards Berlin-Pankow to station Berlin Ostkreuz and change there to line S7 heading towards Postdam Hbf and leave at Alexanderplatz station. Journey time approx. 40 min.

Train

From Berlin Central Station

Take S-Bahn line S5 heading towards Strausberg Nord, S7 heading towards Ahrensfelde, Wartenberg or S9 heading towards Berlin-Schonefeld to Alexanderplatz station. Journey time approx. 7 min.

Taxi

  • From Berlin-Tegel Airport TXL: Journey time approx. 30 min.
  • From Berlin-Schonefeld Airport SXF: Journey time approx. 40 min.
  • From Berlin Central Station: Journey time approx. 10 min.
  • From station BERLIN-FRIEDRICHSTRASSE: Journey time approx. 4 min.

Road

Address for navigation: Alexanderstraße 2, 10178 Berlin

From North

Take the A114 motorway towards Berlin-Zentrum / Berlin-Pankow. Follow the B109 / Prenzlauer Allee and later the Karl-Liebknecht-Strasse towards the city center for about 7 kilometers. Turn left into Alexanderstrasse just before reaching Alexanderplatz square. The entrance to the parking garage is on the right just in front of the hotel.

From East

Take the A100 to exit 20 Tempelhofer Damm and follow the B96 / Tempelhofer Damm towards Kreuzberg and later straight on Wilhelmstrasse. After approx. 3.2 km turn right into Kochstrasse. At the next crossing turn left into Friedrichstrasse and follow the road for about 1 kilometer. Then turn right into the boulevard Unter den Linden and continue along Karl-Liebknecht-Strasse. After another approx. 1.8 km turn right into Alexanderstrasse. The entrance to the public parking garage is on the right just in front of the hotel.

From South & West

Take the A100 motorway towards Beusselstraße until the end and continue on Seestrasse. After about 2 km turn right into Mullerstraße. Follow the road straight on Chausseestrasse and Friedrichstrasse for about 3 km towards Berlin-Mitte. At the corner of Unter den Linden Boulevard turn left and continue along Karl-Liebknecht-Strasse. After another approx. 1.8 km turn right into Alexanderstrasse. The entrance to the parking garage is on the right just next to the hotel.
